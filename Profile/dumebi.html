About My School

The name of my school is Bishop Okoye Memorial Girls Secondary School. My school is located in Osigbo Ukpor in Nnewi South Local Government Area. My beloved school is a Catholic school and also a mission school. My school has great teachers dedicated to their duties and young gifted girls ready to learn.
My school is beautified with lovely flowers and it is meant only for girls who wants to build their academic skill and develop their moral habit. My school sees that every one is carried along when it comes to teaching. It is owned and managed by the Daughters of Divine Love Congeretion. Its building is painted with two colours which are milk and oxblood red. It has well equiped facilities for learning and other activities needed for boarders specifically. My school has 10 classrooms, 37 academic and non-academic staff.
Bishop Okoye Memorial Girls Secondary School indulges in so many exciting activities that will inspire and develop the students, these activities include both indoor and outdoor such as excursion, quiz and debate competitions, parade, relay race and many more. There are recreational activities such as table tennis, football, volleyball, badminton and other activities. The students indulge ludo game, skipping, blind folding, scrabble and any other fun game students can create because we are trained to be creative. The students of my school read not only to pass but to learn and defend themselves in public. Some strict warnings which are also in the school's rules and regulations diary, which every student of BOMGSS must adhere to, such rules include:
  • NO EXAMINATION MALPRACTICE
  • NO BULLYING
  • BE WHERE OTHERS ARE
  • DO NOT LIE ON ANYBODY'S BED
  • DO NOT LOOK DOWN ON ANYBODY NO MATTER THE CRICUMSTANCES.
The students are well taken care of and also contented. There is always a school gatherng where the school principal asks the students questions on how their lessons for the week went,if there is anywhere they find difficult to assimilate maybe from the staff teaching, the student's concentration and understanding towards the lessons being taught. My school also has a computer laboratory also know as NCC (National Communication Commission) where the students go to learn more by researching on the topics the student finds difficult to understand. We the students also use the projector to watch educative movies like Dr. Ben Carson and Spelling Bees, etc. We also use it for sporting activities when the students watch and perform the action displayed to replace the worn out tissues and exercise the body system.
Every second Sunday is the visiting day, parents and guardians are permitted to come and visit their children. Lastly, my school is a gifted and talented school, fun to stay because it holds a lot of inspiring facilities for boarders.

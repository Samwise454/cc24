About My School

The name of my School is St Paul's Seminary Ukpor, it is located at Umunuko Ukpor. The name of our Rector is Father Very Rev. Peter Uzonna Ezeani, who celebrated his silver jubilee on the 29th of June, 2024. The seminary is a formation house and a training ground for future priest. Our academic block is pink in colour while our hostel block is blue in colour. We have seven father formators in the seminary and 16 assistant formators who help in forming the seminarians. in the seminary seminarians are giving opportunities to learn different skills such as bakery, cobbler, music, book binding and so on. I love my seminary because it is a place where love and charity abides.
